Streetwear Aesthetic

Streetwear is defined by bold graphics, relaxed fits, trendy sneakers, and urban influences. It combines casual, comfortable clothing with elements from skateboarding, hip-hop, and current trends.

Old Money Aesthetic

The "old money" aesthetic is about understated elegance and tradition. It features classic, high-quality clothing and accessories in neutral colors, timeless patterns, and a focus on craftsmanship. The style avoids flashy displays of wealth, favoring subtlety and refinement.

Techwear Aesthetic

The techwear aesthetic features sleek, minimalist design, technical fabrics, functional details, and a monochromatic color palette, blending futuristic style with high performance.
